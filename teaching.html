Teaching statement

I have delivered over 800 hours of undergraduate and postgraduate teaching—in French, English, Czech, and Macedonian—across biology, medicine, and lifescience curricula. My courses are based on active learning - in small groups I use learning through play and flipped classroom methods, while in larger groups and more general audiences I encourage student participation through real time polls and gamification.
